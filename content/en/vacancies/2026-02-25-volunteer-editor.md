The public organization SHUM CAMP brings together specialists from the international OSINT community. We investigate crimes committed during the armed conflict launched by russia against Ukraine, provide consultations to law enforcement and judicial authorities, counter disinformation, and implement projects aimed at protecting human rights.

### **What You Will Be Doing**

* Editing analytical texts, reports, briefs, and explanatory materials;
* Bringing materials into a unified style and structure;
* Working with document templates (reports, analytical notes, internal materials);
* Creating and improving editorial templates for different types of content;
* Reviewing the logic of presentation, clarity of wording, and structural accuracy of texts;
* Preparing official letters, requests, and supporting communication with external partners and organizations.

### **Who We Are Looking For**

* Individuals with a confident command of Ukrainian and English;
* Those who are able to work with analytical and technical texts;
* People attentive to detail and structure;
* Candidates with experience or a strong interest in editing reports, research papers, and analytical materials;
* Skills in working with text editors (Google Docs, Word, etc.); experience with Figma will be an advantage.
