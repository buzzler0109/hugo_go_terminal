Welcome to SHUM

SHUM – is a public organization founded by experts from the international OSINT community.

We use OSINT technologies to investigate war crimes and protect human rights during the armed conflict initiated by russia against Ukraine.

Areas of Expertise

OSINT investigations of war crimes

we collect and analyze open-source data to document russia’s war crimes.

Consulting law enforcement and judicial authorities

we provide evidence and assist in identifying potential criminals.

Countering disinformation and human rights projects

we work to expose fake news and support initiatives aimed at protecting human rights.

Our Goal

  • To collect and analyze evidence of violations of international humanitarian law using OSINT tools;
  • To identify potential criminals;
  • To support law enforcement and judicial authorities by providing consultations and expert analysis.

SHUM team includes not only OSINT researchers but also lawyers, analysts, and international law experts.

Mission

Since the beginning of the russian-ukrainian armed conflict, the international community has repeatedly documented war crimes. However, many human rights violations in occupied territories and combat zones often go unnoticed and never reach the judicial authorities due to a lack of evidence.

We believe -
The stories of war crimes victims must be heard and thoroughly investigated.

Core Principles

Effectiveness

we are results-driven and focused on ensuring legal accountability for those responsible.
